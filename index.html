The Ship That Never Sailed

The Vasa ship draw a lot of people to watch it set sail in Stockholm ,year 1628. The 61meter long ship made it only about 1km from the shore before it dissapered under the waves. As mentioned before the ship was 61meter long and had a width of 10,5meters and a weight of 1300ton, the ship was supposed to be a mighty warship for the king Gustav 2 Adolf to travel in when needed.
